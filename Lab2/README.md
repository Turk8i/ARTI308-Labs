## [Chocolate Sales Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/saidaminsaidaxmadov/chocolate-sales)

This CSV dataset contains detailed transactional records of global chocolate product sales from July 2022 to December 2025

## Machine Learning Problem

**Problem Type**: Regression.

**Target Variable (y)**: Boxes Shipped.

**Model's Goal**: Predict how many boxed will be shipped for a new sales record.
